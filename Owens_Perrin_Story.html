My Story

Hello! My name is Perrin Owens, and I am the creator of Memory Zone. My passion for technology and creating a educational website inspired me to build this platform to make learning fun for kids. Growing up, I always struggled with traditional learning methods. I found it hard to stay engaged, and learning became more of a chore than an adventure. That's why I'm passionate about making education enjoyable for everyone. I wanted to create a space where kids can practice important skills like memory and math all while playing games and enjoying themselves!

What's Next?

I'm constantly working to improve and expand Memory Zone, adding new games and features to ensure children continue to have a positive and enriching learning experience. Stay tuned for more updates and exciting additions!
